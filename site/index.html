How SRS Works

SRS (Simple Rating System) rates teams based on goal differential adjusted for strength of schedule.

  • SRS = MOV + SOS
  • MOV (Margin of Victory) = average goal differential per game
  • SOS (Strength of Schedule) = average SRS of opponents

An average team has an SRS of 0.00. A team with SRS of +1.0 scores about 1 more goal per game than average, accounting for who they've played.
