How SRS Works

SRS (Simple Rating System) rates teams based on point differential adjusted for strength of schedule.

  • SRS = MOV + SOS
  • MOV (Margin of Victory) = average point differential per game
  • SOS (Strength of Schedule) = average SRS of opponents

An average team has an SRS of 0.00. A team with SRS of +5.0 is 5 points per game better than average, accounting for who they've played.

The rating is calculated by solving a system of 30 linear equations simultaneously, ensuring each team's SOS reflects the true strength of their opponents.
